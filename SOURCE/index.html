Scottish Highlands

The Highlands of Scotland are one of the most iconic locations in all of Britain. It has continued to be a number one tourist destination for anyone visiting the Isles. The Highlands feature some of the richest history in Scotland. Through its long standing Pictish heritage and the kingdom of Alba, standing against the endless Viking Hordes, its oppressive years under the tyranny of foreign Kings and the home to the last battle on British soil. The Highlands has a colourful history and has been shared widespread through the world.
